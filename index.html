App Guidelines

1. Matchmaking Modes

• Default (FIFO): Strictly follows arrival order (longest wait first). Ensures newcomers (0 games) play first and strictly avoids immediate 4-player repeat lineups by swapping players if needed.

• FairQ ON: Matches players based on skill level. It takes the longest-waiting player (Anchor) and finds 3 others in the queue with the closest skill ratings.

• GMT ON: Prioritizes competitive matchups. Pairs Top 1 & 2 vs Top 3 & 4.

• RT ON: Uses skill levels defined by dots. Pairs Advance with Beginner vs Advance with Beginner (Rank 1 & 4 vs Rank 2 & 3).

• 1 Dot: Beginner (2.0)

• 2 Dots: Novice (3.0)

• 3 Dots: Intermediate (3.5)

• 4 Dots: Advanced (4.5)

2. Player Management

• Profiles: Tap any player in the roster to view all-time vs session stats, change photos, or update skill ratings.

• Idle Mode: Tap the icon to pause a player's wait time without checking them out. Perfect for breaks.

• Check-In: New players are added via the top bar. You can search the Global DB to pull existing players into a new session.

3. Court Operations

• Staging: Matches are first created in the "Up Next Deck". This allows you to review and Shuffle or Edit lineups before they go live.

• Auto-Fill: By default, you must explicitly confirm a staged match to a court. Toggle "Locked/Unlocked" to manage staging capacity.

• Live Edits: Need to swap a player mid-game? Use "Change / Auto Replace" on any occupied court.

4. Session & Data

• Export: "Export Summary" generates a professional CSV or PDF containing leaderboards, wait time analysis, and full match logs.

• Reset: Use "RESET SYSTEM" only at the end of a major event. It clears local session data but preserves the Global Player Database.

• Sync: The app works 100% offline. Data syncs to the global cloud automatically when a connection is detected.

5. iOS Installation

• Add to Home Screen: Open this link in Safari, tap the Share icon (square with arrow), and select "Add to Home Screen" for a full-screen app experience.

• Offline Use: Once added, the app works entirely offline. All data is saved locally to your device.
