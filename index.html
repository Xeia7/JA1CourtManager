App Guidelines

1. Matchmaking Modes

• GMT ON: Prioritizes competitive matchups. Pairs Top 1 & 2 vs Top 3 & 4.

• RT ON: Uses skill levels defined by dots:

• 1 Dot: Beginner (2.0)

• 2 Dots: Beginning Novice (2.5)

• 3 Dots: Novice (3.0)

• 4 Dots: Intermediate (3.5)

• 5 Dots: Advanced Intermediate (4.0)

• 6 Dots: Advanced (4.5)

• FairQ: Rotates players based on wait time and least games played.

2. Player Profiles

• Edit Profile: Tap any player in the roster to change their photo or rating.

• All-Time Stats: View global records in the Global Stats tab.

3. Offline Support

• This app works completely offline. Your data is stored on this device.
